MyCity App LS2

The App provides a one stop solution for all the problem an individual might face in a city a tourist or otherwise .It includes all the generic amenities an individual may fall in need of like the cities main attractions,hotels,
emergency contact numbers,Schools,colleges and police stations,public representatives,local train information ,bus timing and many more .

Installation of Android Studio and prerequisite knowledge of java must be required.

The data is fetched from various apis (mostly google).

(No  external dependencies required)
